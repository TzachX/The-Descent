GameManager - Running all the other managers, doesn't have a specific functunality

LevelGenerator - Creates the levels, holds the data for the levels

UIManager - The only one that holds the UI components and activate the delegates for them

TurnManager - Responsible for managing the turns and checking who won

InputManager - Checks the input and activate the functions accordingly

SpawnManager - Spawns everything that is not the level itself (basically, the player and enemies)
